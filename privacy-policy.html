1. Introduction

ORYN Inc. ("we," "our," or "us"), developed by HamedMohammed, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use BookAPT, our mobile application ("App", "BookAPT", "the Service"), as well as our websites and software products (collectively, the "Services").

BookAPT is a smart appointment booking platform mobile application developed by HamedMohammed and operated by ORYN Inc. This Privacy Policy specifically applies to the BookAPT mobile application available on Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

By using BookAPT or our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide (name, email, phone, address) and information collected automatically (device info, usage data, location).

Location Data Collection

By providing your zipcode/postal code and address during registration, you consent to our collection and use of location data for the following purposes:

  • Business Search: We use your zipcode to convert it to geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) using geocoding services to help you find nearby businesses and services
  • Third-Party Services: We share your zipcode (converted to coordinates) with Foursquare Places API to search for businesses near your location that may not yet be registered on BookAPT
  • Service Improvement: Location data helps us personalize your experience and show relevant businesses in your area

You can update or delete your location information at any time through your Profile Settings.

Geocoding Services: We use third-party geocoding services (Nominatim/OpenStreetMap and optionally Google Geocoding API) to convert your zipcode to coordinates. These services process your zipcode but do not receive other personal information.

Foursquare API: We use Foursquare Places API to search for businesses. When you search for businesses, your zipcode (converted to coordinates) is sent to Foursquare to find nearby businesses. Foursquare's use of this data is governed by their privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process and manage appointments, bookings, and transactions
  • Send you appointment reminders, confirmations, and notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Find nearby businesses: Use your zipcode to search for businesses and services in your area using Foursquare Places API
  • Location-based recommendations: Show you relevant businesses based on your location
