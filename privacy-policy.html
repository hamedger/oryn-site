1. Introduction

Oryn Inc. ("we," "our," or "us"), operating as OrynSolutions and developed by HamedMohammed, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use BookAPT, our mobile application ("App", "BookAPT", "the Service"), as well as our websites and software products (collectively, the "Services").

BookAPT is a smart appointment booking platform mobile application developed by HamedMohammed and operated by Oryn Inc./OrynSolutions. This Privacy Policy specifically applies to the BookAPT mobile application available on Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

By using BookAPT or our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password, and other registration details
  • Business Information: Company name, business type, address, and other business details
  • Appointment Data: Booking details, service preferences, appointment history
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communication Data: Messages, support requests, feedback, and other communications

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you use our Services:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, interaction patterns
  • Location Data:
    • GPS coordinates (if location services are enabled and permission is granted)
    • IP-based location information
    • Zipcode/Postal Code: We collect your zipcode/postal code during account registration, which is required to provide location-based services such as finding nearby businesses
    • Geographic Coordinates: We convert your zipcode to latitude/longitude coordinates using geocoding services (Nominatim/OpenStreetMap or Google Geocoding API) to enable business search functionality
    • Address information you provide during registration
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, referring URLs

Location Data Collection and Consent:

By providing your zipcode/postal code and address during registration, you consent to:

  • Collection of your zipcode and address information
  • Conversion of your zipcode to geographic coordinates using geocoding services
  • Sharing of location coordinates with Foursquare Places API to search for nearby businesses that may not yet be registered on our platform
  • Use of location data to provide location-based services and business recommendations

You can update or delete your location information at any time through your Profile Settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process and manage appointments, bookings, and transactions
  • Send you appointment reminders, confirmations, and notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Find nearby businesses: Use your zipcode to search for businesses and services in your area using Foursquare Places API
  • Location-based recommendations: Show you relevant businesses based on your location

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)

  • Email and SMS delivery services
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

  • Location and Business Search Services:
    • Foursquare Places API: We share your zipcode (converted to geographic coordinates) with Foursquare to search for businesses near your location. Foursquare uses this data to return relevant business listings. Their use of this data is governed by Foursquare's privacy policy.
    • Geocoding Services: We use Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) and optionally Google Geocoding API to convert zipcodes to coordinates. These services process your zipcode but do not receive other personal information.

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.
