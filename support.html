Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Download the BookAPT app from the App Store or Google Play, or visit our web platform. Click "Sign Up" and follow the registration process.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! BookAPT offers a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required.

Bookings & Appointments

How do I book an appointment?

You can book in two ways: Use our 30-second visual booking flow or chat naturally with our AI assistant to find and book services.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes! Open your appointment in the app and use the "Reschedule" or "Cancel" options. You'll receive confirmation notifications.

Payment & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Venmo, and other secure payment options.

Are there transaction fees?

No! BookAPT has zero transaction fees. Just one simple monthly subscription.

Privacy & Security

How is my data protected?

We use enterprise-grade encryption (HTTPS/SSL), secure authentication, and comply with industry security standards. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Contact us at info@orynsolutions.io to request account deletion.
