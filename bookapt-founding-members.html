What is a Founding Member?

A Founding Member is one of the first 200 businesses to join BookAPT. You get 50% off your first year and lock in this price for 5 years.

What do I get with the Founding Member plan?

You get the full BookAPT Professional plan ($59/month value) for just $14.99/month for your first year. That's 75% off! This includes up to 200 appointments/month, advanced scheduling, worker management, payment processing, analytics, and all Professional features.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel anytime. However, if you cancel, you'll lose your Founding Member discount. We're confident you'll love BookAPT!

What happens after the first year?

After your first year, you'll continue to pay the discounted Founding Member rate ($14.99/month) for 5 years total. After 5 years, you'll have the option to renew at a discounted rate.

How long is the price locked?

Your Founding Member price is locked for 5 years from your signup date. After 5 years, you'll have the option to renew at a discounted rate to continue enjoying savings.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees, no hidden costs. Just $179.94 for your first year (paid upfront) or $14.99/month if you prefer monthly billing.

How do I install the widget?

It takes just 2 minutes! We'll send you a simple code to paste into your website. No coding knowledge required. We also provide step-by-step guides for WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, and more.

What if I need help?

Founding Members get priority support. You can reach us via email, chat, or phone. We're here to help you succeed!
